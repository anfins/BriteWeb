College is hard,

we understand.

Scroll

While earning our engineering degrees, we sat in many classrooms where we were often too afraid to ask our questions aloud because we thought they were "dumb questions". So, we held back from asking which diminished our ability to learn. We were fortunate enough to make it through university, but many students aren't.

Additionally, we are only able to provide our professors with quantitative student-feedback once at the very end of each semester through our end of the semester course evals. We realized that by the time professors receive this feedback (after the semester is over), the students who might have benefitted from a change in the course's pace or structure had either already failed, or dropped the course. After talking with professors at leading colleges across the country, we learned they were frustrated too.

These familiar challenges inspired us to build Brite to solve the issues we face as students, and to give educators a simple to use platform to gather actionable feedback. Our mission is to empower students to overcome systemic barriers by providing innovative solutions and support. We believe that every student deserves a fair chance at achieving their academic goals, regardless of their background or circumstances. By leveraging technology and data, we aim to create a more equitable and inclusive platform that sets students up for success.

We created Brite to give students a voice in the classroom
