Our Mission

To support students to overcome systemic barriers by providing innovative solutions and support. We believe that every student deserves a fair chance at achieving their academic goals, regardless of their background or circumstances. By leveraging technology and data, we aim to create a more equitable and inclusive platform that sets students up for success.

Built By Current Students

College is hard, we understand. While earning our engineering degrees, we sat in many classrooms where we were often too afraid to ask our questions aloud because we thought they were "dumb questions". There was also no easy way to alert our teacher with our feedback throughout the semester, which also diminished our learning. We were fortunate enough to make it through university, but many students aren't. These familiar challenges inspired us to build an in-class app, Brite, to solve the issues we faced as students, and to give educators an easy to use platform that eases their problems.
